## Getting Started

### Creating Your Company Account

1. Visit [https://trm.com/company/signup](https://trm.com/company/signup)
2. Fill in company details:
   - Company Name (as registered)
   - Business Email
   - Contact Phone
   - Industry
   - Company Size
3. Create admin account:
   - Admin Name
   - Admin Email
   - Password
4. Verify email address
5. Complete company profile

### Verification Process

To ensure platform quality, all companies must be verified:

#### Required Documents
- Business Registration Certificate
- Company Tax ID
- Official company letterhead
- Proof of address (utility bill or lease)

#### Verification Steps
1. Upload documents in Company Settings
2. Our team reviews within 2-3 business days
3. Receive approval notification
4. Start posting jobs!

### Your First Login

After verification, you'll see:

- **Company Dashboard**: Overview of all activities
- **Job Listings**: Manage your open positions
- **Referrals**: Review candidate referrals
- **Team**: Manage company users
- **Billing**: Subscription and payment management
- **Analytics**: Performance insights

## Understanding the Platform

### What is TRM?

TRM (Talent Referral Marketplace) is Myanmar's premier referral-based hiring platform. Instead of traditional job boards, we leverage a network of professional referrers who recommend qualified candidates from their networks.

### How It Works

```
┌─────────────┐     ┌─────────────┐     ┌─────────────┐
│   Company   │────▶│    Post     │────▶│   Set Bonus │
│             │     │    Job      │     │   Amount    │
└─────────────┘     └─────────────┘     └─────────────┘
                                               │
                                               ▼
┌─────────────┐     ┌─────────────┐     ┌─────────────┐
│   Hire      │◀────│   Review    │◀────│  Referrer   │
│ Candidate   │     │ Candidates  │     │  Submits    │
└─────────────┘     └─────────────┘     │  Referral   │
                                        └─────────────┘
```

### Business Model

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Subscription** | Monthly fee based on tier (Starter/Growth/Enterprise) |
| **Referral Bonus** | You set the amount (paid only on successful hire) |
| **Platform Fee** | 15% of referral bonus (taken from bonus, not extra) |
| **Per-Hire Fee** | 50,000 MMK per successful hire |

### Subscription Tiers

## Posting Jobs

### Creating a Job Posting

#### Step 1: Basic Information
- **Job Title**: Clear and specific
- **Department**: Internal organization
- **Employment Type**: Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Internship
- **Location**: Onsite, Remote, or Hybrid
- **Salary Range**: Competitive range in MMK

#### Step 2: Job Details

**Description** (500-2000 words):
- Company overview
- Role summary
- Team structure
- Growth opportunities

**Requirements**:
- Education level
- Years of experience
- Technical skills
- Soft skills
- Language requirements

**Responsibilities**:
- Day-to-day tasks
- Key deliverables
- Reporting structure
- Success metrics

**Benefits**:
- Salary and bonuses
- Health insurance
- Leave policy
- Professional development
- Perks

#### Step 3: Referral Settings

**Referral Bonus**:
- Minimum: 50,000 MMK
- Recommended: 100,000-500,000 MMK based on role seniority
- Higher bonuses attract more quality referrers

**Bonus Calculation Example**:
```
Referral Bonus: 200,000 MMK
Platform Fee (15%): 30,000 MMK
Referrer Receives: 170,000 MMK
Your Total Cost: 200,000 MMK + 50,000 MMK (per-hire fee) = 250,000 MMK
```

#### Step 4: Application Settings

**Requirements**:
- Resume required: Yes/No
- Cover letter: Required/Optional/Not needed
- Portfolio: For creative roles
- Custom questions: Up to 5

**Screening Questions**:
- Years of experience
- Salary expectations
- Availability
- Specific skills
- Relocation willingness

### Job Status Management

| Status | Description | Visibility |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| **Draft** | Working on posting | Only you |
| **Active** | Live and accepting referrals | Public |
| **Paused** | Temporarily not accepting | Hidden |
| **Closed** | No longer accepting | Hidden |
| **Filled** | Position filled | Hidden |

### Featured Jobs

## Managing Referrals

### Referral Workflow

```
Submitted → Under Review → Shortlisted → Interview → Offer → Hired
```

### Reviewing Referrals

#### Dashboard View
- New referrals highlighted
- Status filters
- Sort by date, bonus amount, or status
- Bulk actions

#### Referral Details
Each referral shows:
- Candidate information
- Resume and documents
- Referrer notes
- Source tracking
- Status history

### Status Updates

Update candidates promptly:

1. **Submitted** → **Under Review**
   - Initial screening
   - Check qualifications match

2. **Under Review** → **Shortlisted**
   - Candidate meets requirements
   - Moving to next stage

3. **Shortlisted** → **Interview Scheduled**
   - Set interview date
   - Notify candidate and referrer

4. **Interview Scheduled** → **Interview Completed**
   - Post-interview evaluation

5. **Interview Completed** → **Offer Extended**
   - Decision made
   - Offer sent

6. **Offer Extended** → **Hired**
   - Candidate accepted
   - Process payout

7. **Any Stage** → **Rejected**
   - Provide reason (optional but recommended)

### Communication

#### With Referrers
- Status updates automatic
- Can add notes for context
- Respond to questions

#### With Candidates
- Interview scheduling
- Feedback provision
- Offer negotiation

### Best Practices for Review

✅ **Do**:
- Review referrals within 48 hours
- Provide feedback on rejections
- Keep referrers informed
- Be respectful to all candidates
- Document reasons for decisions

❌ **Don't**:
- Leave referrals in "Submitted" too long
- Ghost candidates or referrers
- Reject without reason
- Change bonus after hire
- Discriminate unfairly

### Duplicate Handling

System automatically detects:
- Same email address
- Same phone number
- Similar resume content

You'll be notified of potential duplicates to avoid double payment.

## Billing & Subscriptions

### Subscription Management

#### Current Plan
View in **Billing** → **Subscription**:
- Plan name and price
- Billing cycle
- Next renewal date
- Usage (jobs posted, team members)

#### Upgrading
1. Go to **Billing** → **Upgrade**
2. Select new plan
3. Review prorated charges
4. Confirm upgrade
5. Immediate access to new features

#### Downgrading
1. Go to **Billing** → **Change Plan**
2. Select lower plan
3. Changes effective at next billing cycle
4. Must reduce usage to new limits

#### Cancellation
1. Go to **Billing** → **Cancel**
2. Provide reason (helps us improve)
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Access continues until period end

### Payment Methods

#### Supported Methods
- **KBZPay**: Instant, no fees
- **WavePay**: Instant, no fees
- **CB Pay**: Instant, no fees
- **Bank Transfer**: 1-2 days, 500 MMK fee
- **Credit/Debit Card**: Visa, Mastercard

#### Adding Payment Method
1. Go to **Billing** → **Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Method**
3. Enter details
4. Set as default (optional)

#### Automatic Payments
Enable auto-pay to avoid service interruption:
- Charged 3 days before due date
- Uses default payment method
- Email receipt sent
- Can disable anytime

### Invoices

#### Viewing Invoices
All invoices in **Billing** → **Invoices**:
- Subscription charges
- Per-hire fees
- Prorated adjustments
- Tax invoices

#### Invoice Details
Each invoice includes:
- Invoice number
- Date issued
- Due date
- Line items
- Subtotal and tax
- Total amount
- Payment status

#### Downloading
- PDF format
- Myanmar tax compliant
- Available for 7 years

### Referral Bonus Payments

#### How It Works
1. Candidate marked as "Hired"
2. 30-day guarantee period starts
3. After guarantee period, bonus is due
4. System generates invoice
5. Pay via available methods
6. Referrer receives payment

#### Payment Timing
- **Immediate**: Pay anytime after hire
- **Consolidated**: Monthly batch payment
- **Auto-Pay**: Set up automatic payment

#### Per-Hire Fee
- 50,000 MMK per successful hire
- Billed monthly or immediately
- Separate from referral bonus

## Best Practices

### Writing Effective Job Posts

#### Title Best Practices
✅ Good: "Senior React Developer - FinTech Startup"
❌ Bad: "Developer Needed"

✅ Good: "Marketing Manager (5+ years) - Consumer Goods"
❌ Bad: "Looking for Marketing Person"

#### Description Tips
1. **Start with Hook**: Why is this role exciting?
2. **Be Specific**: Avoid vague requirements
3. **Show Culture**: What makes your company unique?
4. **Be Honest**: Don't oversell or undersell
5. **Use Bullets**: Easy to scan
6. **Include Growth**: Career progression path

#### Setting Referral Bonuses

**Recommended Formula**:
```
Base Bonus = 1 month salary (minimum)
Senior Roles = 1.5-2 months salary
Executive Roles = 2-3 months salary
Hard-to-Fill = Add 25-50% premium
```

**Examples**:
| Role Level | Monthly Salary | Suggested Bonus |
|------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Junior | 300,000 MMK | 100,000-150,000 MMK |
| Mid-Level | 600,000 MMK | 200,000-300,000 MMK |
| Senior | 1,200,000 MMK | 400,000-600,000 MMK |
| Executive | 2,500,000 MMK | 800,000-1,000,000 MMK |

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: Typically 2-3 business days after document submission.

**Q: Can I post jobs before verification?**
A: No, verification is required to ensure platform quality.

**Q: What happens if I don't pay the referral bonus?**
A: Unpaid bonuses affect your company rating and may result in account suspension.

**Q: Can I negotiate the bonus after a referral is submitted?**
A: No, the bonus is fixed at the time of posting. Changes only apply to future referrals.

### Job Posting Questions

**Q: How many jobs can I post?**
A: Depends on your plan: Starter (5), Growth (20), Enterprise (Unlimited).

**Q: Can I edit a job after posting?**
A: Yes, except the referral bonus amount (to protect referrers).

**Q: How long do jobs stay active?**
A: 60 days by default. You can extend or close early.

**Q: Can I repost a closed job?**
A: Yes, create a new posting or reactivate the old one.

### Referral Questions

**Q: What if I already know the candidate?**
A: You can still accept the referral, but you must pay the bonus if hired.

**Q: Can I reject a referral without reason?**
A: While possible, providing reasons improves referrer relationships.

**Q: What if the candidate is a duplicate?**
A: The system detects duplicates. First valid referral gets the bonus.

**Q: How long do I have to review referrals?**
A: No deadline, but prompt review (within 48 hours) is recommended.

### Billing Questions

**Q: Can I get a refund on my subscription?**
A: No refunds, but you can cancel anytime and use until period end.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: KBZPay, WavePay, CB Pay, bank transfer, and credit/debit cards.

**Q: When am I charged the per-hire fee?**
A: Monthly in arrears, or immediately if you prefer.

**Q: Do you provide tax invoices?**
A: Yes, all invoices are Myanmar tax compliant.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Do you have an API?**
A: Yes, available for Growth and Enterprise plans.

**Q: Can I integrate with my ATS?**
A: Yes, we offer integrations with popular ATS systems.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use bank-level encryption and security measures.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, you can export referrals, analytics, and reports.

**Q: How do I close my company account?**
A: Contact support. All pending bonuses must be paid before closure.
