## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit [https://trm.com](https://trm.com) and click "Sign Up"
2. Select **"I want to refer candidates"** as your role
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name (as shown on your NRC)
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number (Myanmar format: 09xxxxxxxxx)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Verify your email address via the confirmation link
5. Complete your profile with a professional photo

### Understanding Your Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see:

- **Available Balance**: Money ready to withdraw
- **Pending Balance**: Earnings from referrals in progress
- **Total Earnings**: All-time earnings from successful hires
- **Active Referrals**: Candidates you've referred currently in the pipeline
- **Successful Hires**: Candidates who got hired through your referrals

## Understanding the Platform

### What is TRM?

TRM (Talent Referral Marketplace) connects talented individuals with great job opportunities. As a referrer, you:

- Browse job openings from verified companies
- Refer candidates from your network
- Earn commission when your referrals get hired
- Get paid directly to your KBZPay, WavePay, or bank account

### How Referral Bonuses Work

Each job posting shows a **Referral Bonus** amount. This is what you can earn:

| Stage | What Happens | Your Earnings |
|-------|--------------|---------------|
| You Refer | Submit candidate details | - |
| Company Reviews | Company evaluates candidate | - |
| Candidate Hired | Candidate accepts job offer | **85% of bonus** |
| Platform Fee | TRM commission | 15% of bonus |

**Example**: If a job has a 200,000 MMK referral bonus:
- You earn: 170,000 MMK (85%)
- Platform fee: 30,000 MMK (15%)

### Referral Status Meanings

Track your referrals through these stages:

1. **Submitted** - Referral received, awaiting review
2. **Under Review** - Company is evaluating the candidate
3. **Shortlisted** - Candidate passed initial screening
4. **Interview Scheduled** - Interview date set
5. **Interview Completed** - Interview finished, awaiting decision
6. **Offer Extended** - Job offer sent to candidate
7. **Hired** üéâ - Candidate accepted offer (you earn!)
8. **Rejected** - Candidate not selected
9. **Paid** üí∞ - Bonus transferred to your account

## Submitting Referrals

### Finding Jobs to Refer

1. **Browse Jobs**: Visit the Jobs page to see all open positions
2. **Filter by**: 
   - Location (Yangon, Mandalay, Remote, etc.)
   - Job Type (Full-time, Part-time, Contract)
   - Industry (Tech, Finance, Healthcare, etc.)
   - Referral Bonus amount
3. **Search**: Use keywords to find specific roles

### Making a Quality Referral

#### Step 1: Select the Right Job
- Ensure the candidate's skills match the requirements
- Check the candidate is genuinely interested
- Verify they haven't applied directly already

#### Step 2: Gather Information
You'll need:
- Candidate's full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Current job title
- Resume (PDF format, max 10MB)

#### Step 3: Submit the Referral

1. Click **"Refer Candidate"** on the job page
2. Fill in candidate details
3. Upload their resume
4. Add a personal note (optional but recommended)
5. Click **"Submit Referral"**

#### Step 4: Share the Referral Link

After submission, you'll get a unique referral link. Share it with the candidate so they can:
- Track their application status
- Upload additional documents if needed
- Communicate with the company

### Referral Limits

- **Free Tier**: 5 referrals per month
- **Silver Tier**: 20 referrals per month
- **Gold Tier**: 50 referrals per month
- **Platinum Tier**: Unlimited referrals

Upgrade your tier by referring more candidates and earning points!

## Earnings & Payouts

### Understanding Your Balances

#### Available Balance
- Money ready to withdraw
- Minimum withdrawal: 10,000 MMK
- Comes from referrals marked as "Hired" and processed

#### Pending Balance
- Earnings from candidates in the hiring process
- Moves to Available when candidate is hired and guarantee period passes

#### Total Earnings
- Cumulative earnings since joining
- Includes all paid and pending amounts

### Requesting a Payout

#### Requirements
1. KYC verification completed ‚úì
2. Minimum balance of 10,000 MMK ‚úì
3. Valid payment method added ‚úì

#### Supported Payment Methods

| Method | Processing Time | Fees |
|--------|----------------|------|
| KBZPay | 1-2 business days | Free |
| WavePay | 1-2 business days | Free |
| CB Pay | 1-2 business days | Free |
| Bank Transfer | 2-3 business days | 500 MMK |

#### How to Request Payout

1. Go to **Earnings** ‚Üí **Payouts**
2. Click **"Request Payout"**
3. Enter amount (or select "All Available")
4. Choose payment method
5. Confirm details
6. Submit request

#### Payout Timeline

1. **Request Submitted** - You submit payout request
2. **Under Review** - Admin reviews (usually within 24 hours)
3. **Processing** - Payment being sent
4. **Completed** - Money in your account!

### Tax Information

- Earnings are subject to Myanmar income tax
- You'll receive an annual tax statement
- Consult a tax professional for advice

## KYC Verification

### Why KYC is Required

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is required by Myanmar financial regulations and to:
- Prevent fraud
- Ensure secure payouts
- Comply with anti-money laundering laws
- Protect your earnings

### Required Documents

You must provide:

1. **NRC Front** - Clear photo of front side
2. **NRC Back** - Clear photo of back side
3. **Selfie** - Photo holding your NRC next to your face

### Document Requirements

- Photos must be clear and legible
- All corners of NRC must be visible
- Selfie must show your face and NRC clearly
- File format: JPG, PNG (max 5MB each)

### How to Submit

1. Go to **Profile** ‚Üí **Verification**
2. Upload NRC Front
3. Upload NRC Back
4. Take/upload selfie
5. Submit for review

### Verification Timeline

- **Submission**: Immediate
- **Review**: 1-3 business days
- **Approval/Rejection**: Email notification sent

If rejected, you'll receive feedback on what needs correction.

### Privacy & Security

- Documents are encrypted and stored securely
- Only authorized staff can access them
- Documents are never shared with third parties
- You can request deletion after account closure

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is TRM free to use?**
A: Yes! It's completely free for referrers. We only charge companies.

**Q: Can I refer myself for a job?**
A: No, self-referrals are not allowed. Use the direct application option instead.

**Q: How long do referrals stay active?**
A: Referrals remain active for 90 days. If no decision is made, they expire.

**Q: Can I edit a referral after submitting?**
A: You can add notes, but cannot change candidate details. Contact support for corrections.

### Earnings Questions

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: After a candidate is hired and completes any guarantee period (usually 30 days).

**Q: What if the company doesn't pay?**
A: We verify companies before they can post jobs. If payment issues occur, we mediate and protect your earnings.

**Q: Can I get paid in USD?**
A: Currently, all payouts are in Myanmar Kyat (MMK).

**Q: Is there a maximum payout amount?**
A: No limit! The more successful referrals, the more you earn.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What file formats are supported for resumes?**
A: PDF only, maximum 10MB.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

**Q: Can I use TRM on mobile?**
A: Yes! Our mobile app is available for iOS and Android, or use the mobile-responsive website.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest 2 versions).

### Account Questions

**Q: Can I change my role from referrer to job seeker?**
A: Yes, contact support to request a role change.

**Q: What happens if I close my account?**
A: Any pending payouts will still be processed. You can reopen your account within 30 days.

**Q: Can I have multiple referrer accounts?**
A: No, one account per person. Multiple accounts will be suspended.

**Q: How do I update my payment information?**
A: Go to Settings ‚Üí Payment Methods to add or update your payment details.
